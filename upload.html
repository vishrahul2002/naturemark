Instructions for Uploading Your Data File

Before uploading your file, please ensure it meets the following criteria to ensure a smooth and efficient processing experience:

  1. Required Data Columns: Your data should be organized into the following columns in the specified order:
  2. Location: The specific location where the data was collected.
  3. Date: The date when the data was collected (format: DD-MM-YYYY).
  4. Scientific Name: The scientific name of the species observed.
  5. Height: The height of the plant or tree measured (in meters).
  6. Width: The width of the plant or tree at its widest point (in meters).
  7. Diameter: The diameter of the stem or trunk (in centimeters).
  8. Carbon Seq: The amount of carbon sequestration (in kilograms or appropriate unit).
  9. Latitude: The latitude of the location where the data was collected..
  10. Longitude: The longitude of the location where the data was collected.

Average Height

This gives a detailed graph showing the average height of the trees for each species. The total height of the tree for each species is taken and divided by the total number of trees for that particular species to determine a height estimate. It also shows the tallest tree and also the shortest tree.
